# TrainQuest Agent Homepage Visual Directions

These are static cover concepts for the next homepage redesign. They do not change app code.

## Direction A: Hearth Gym

Theme source: custom `theme-factory` direction inspired by Golden Hour + Forest Canopy.

Purpose: make the app feel like a warm pixel RPG training room instead of a cold dashboard.

Palette:
- Background charcoal: `#15100E`
- Panel brown: `#241914`
- Warm floor: `#5B3927`
- Amber light: `#F0A64A`
- Ember accent: `#D16A43`
- Soft teal accent: `#67B3A2`
- Cream text: `#F4DFC1`

Use when: you want stronger RPG identity, nighttime training mood, and a cozy "my character is waiting" feeling.

## Direction B: Morning Dojo

Theme source: custom `theme-factory` direction inspired by Botanical Garden + Desert Rose.

Purpose: make the app feel calmer, gentler, and closer to Forest / focus-timer companionship.

Palette:
- Deep green charcoal: `#121B17`
- Dojo wall: `#243329`
- Moss shadow: `#314637`
- Morning cream: `#EFE3C8`
- Soft amber: `#D8A85C`
- Terracotta accent: `#C46E52`
- Gentle teal: `#7BB8A3`

Use when: you want lower pressure, softer daily-open appeal, and less "game UI intensity".

## Recommendation

Start with Direction A's scene structure and Direction B's calmer warmth:
- Keep the pixel training room and platform.
- Reduce purple dominance.
- Make the record button feel like part of the scene.
- Keep data hidden until user asks for it.
